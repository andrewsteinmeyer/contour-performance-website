Concierge Medicine

A Retainer Model for Your Health

Dr. Ed O'Bryan · April 7, 2026 · 6 min read

Your health is your most valuable asset. Like any high-value asset, it requires:

  • Ongoing oversight
  • Strategic decision-making
  • Immediate access when needed

Our model is built on a monthly retainer, giving you direct access to a physician who understands your full picture and manages your health proactively—not reactively. You're not paying for appointments. You're investing in how you operate.

Why Concierge Performance Medicine?

Traditional care is fragmented: Long wait times. Short appointments. Multiple providers with no coordination. We replace that with a single, integrated system.

  • Minimal wait times, extended consults
  • Same or next-day availability when needed
  • Direct physician access via phone, text, or video
  • Seamless referrals to top specialists when necessary
  • All coordinated under one physician who knows you

The Experience

Every member receives a level of attention and access that is not possible in traditional medicine.

  • Comprehensive, in-depth health assessments
  • Personalized performance and longevity strategy
  • Ongoing protocol refinement based on real data
  • Continuous oversight—not just episodic visits
  • Integration of nutrition, fitness, recovery, and medical care

This is whole-person optimization, not symptom management.

Built for a Select Group

We intentionally limit our panel to ensure quality over volume. This allows the physician to spend meaningful time with each patient and deliver a truly personalized experience.

Member Benefits

As a member, you receive:

  • Direct, ongoing access to your physician
  • Longer, unrushed consultations
  • Proactive health and performance management
  • Advanced diagnostics and cutting-edge therapies
  • Coordinated care across all aspects of your health
  • A simplified, streamlined medical experience

Compatible With Your Insurance

Our services are designed to complement, not replace, your existing insurance coverage. You can continue to use insurance for:

  • Specialist visits
  • Prescriptions
  • Imaging
  • Therapy services
  • Hospital-based care

We focus on optimization, prevention, and personalized oversight—areas where traditional systems often fall short.

This isn't anything you're used to.
