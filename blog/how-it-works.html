Your Path Forward

How It Works

Dr. Ed O'Bryan · April 7, 2026 · 4 min read

Becoming a member of Contour Performance Health is a deliberate process — by design. We don't believe in rushing into a healthcare relationship any more than you'd rush into a business partnership. The process is simple, transparent, and built around mutual fit.

Here's exactly how it works, from the first conversation to ongoing care.

1. Discovery Call

Everything begins with a complimentary discovery call. This is a two-way conversation — not a sales pitch — designed to determine whether working together makes sense for both sides.

During the discovery call, we'll:

  • Understand your goals, lifestyle, and expectations
  • Explain our approach in more detail
  • Determine if this is the right fit for both sides

There's no pressure and no obligation. This is about performance, and your time is valuable. If the fit isn't right, we'll tell you — and if there's a better resource for what you need, we'll point you in that direction.

Schedule your complimentary discovery call →

2. Initial Consultation

If we're aligned after the discovery call, we'll schedule your initial consultation — a 60 to 90 minute deep dive into your health, performance, and long-term objectives. This is where we start building the foundation of your personalized strategy.

During the initial consultation, we assess:

  • Current health status and history
  • Training, nutrition, recovery, and lifestyle
  • Biomarkers and any prior testing
  • Short- and long-term performance goals

This isn't a surface-level intake. It's a thorough evaluation that gives Dr. O'Bryan the context needed to understand where you are, where you want to go, and what's standing in the way.

From there, we begin building your personalized health and performance strategy — grounded in functional medicine, informed by advanced diagnostics, and designed for long-term optimization.

3. Ongoing Membership

Following your initial consultation, you'll transition into membership. This is where the real value compounds.

Health optimization isn't a one-time event — it's an ongoing process. Your body changes, your goals evolve, and the data tells a story that unfolds over time. Membership gives you the continuity and access needed to stay ahead of the curve.

As a member, you receive:

  • Continuous physician oversight
  • Ongoing optimization and refinement of your health strategy
  • Direct access when you need it
  • Long-term performance and longevity strategy

This is the retainer model applied to your health — a sustained partnership built around your goals, not around billing codes or appointment slots.

No Obligations

We believe the work should speak for itself. There are no long-term contracts, no hidden commitments, and no pressure to stay if it's not working for you. This is about performance, and your time is valuable.

The people who stay — and most do — stay because the results are real, the access is unmatched, and the relationship makes their life measurably better.

If you're ready to find out whether Contour Performance is the right fit, the first step is simple: schedule a discovery call.
