Is This Right for You?

Who Is a Performance Medicine Membership Right For?

Dr. Ed O'Bryan · April 2, 2026 · 5 min read

Not everyone is looking for the same thing from their healthcare. Some people want the standard fifteen-minute visit — quick, efficient, move on. And that's fine. That model serves a purpose.

But if you're reading this, you're probably not that person. You're probably someone who's been looking for something more — something that matches the level of intention you bring to every other important area of your life.

At Contour Performance Health, we built a membership-based performance medicine practice for a specific kind of person. Here's how to know if that person is you.

You Want Real Results — and You're Willing to Do the Work

Let's be straightforward: this membership isn't for people looking for a quick fix. It's for people who understand that meaningful, lasting improvements in health require engagement, consistency, and partnership with a physician who's equally invested.

If you've ever felt frustrated because your healthcare felt passive — like you were being managed rather than empowered — this approach will feel different. We don't just hand you a plan and send you on your way. We build it together, adjust it based on real data, and hold you accountable along the way.

The people who get the most out of this membership are the ones who:

  • Show up ready to be honest about where they are and where they want to be
  • Are willing to make changes — in nutrition, movement, sleep, stress management, or other areas — when the data says it matters
  • Value depth over convenience and understand that real progress takes time
  • Want a physician who will challenge them, not just agree with them

This is healthcare for people who treat their health the way they treat their career, their fitness, their finances — with intention, strategy, and follow-through.

You Want to Be Healthier Longer — Starting Now

There's a fundamental difference between waiting for something to go wrong and actively working to stay well. If you're the kind of person who thinks proactively about your future — who invests in tomorrow rather than just reacting to today — this membership was designed for you.

Longevity medicine isn't about adding years to your life at the expense of quality. It's about extending your healthspan — the years you spend feeling energized, sharp, capable, and independent. And the research is clear: the earlier you start making intentional choices about your health, the greater the impact.

This membership is for people who:

  • Don't want to wait until their 60s or 70s to "get serious" about their health
  • Want to understand their body at a deeper level than annual checkups provide
  • Are interested in advanced testing — metabolic profiling, body composition analysis, cardiovascular risk assessment, and more — to make informed, proactive decisions
  • Believe that health is something you build deliberately, not something you hope works out

You don't have to be an elite athlete or a biohacking enthusiast to benefit from this approach. You just have to care enough about your future self to start investing now.

You're Done with Healthcare That Doesn't Work for You

This one's personal for a lot of people — and it's worth being direct about.

If you've been stuck between two broken models, this membership exists because of you.

Frustrated with Conventional Healthcare?

You're not alone. The traditional healthcare system is built for volume, not depth. Appointments are too short. Providers are overloaded. You feel like a number, not a person. The focus is on reacting to problems after they appear rather than working to stay ahead of them.

You know that your health deserves more attention than ten minutes and a generic recommendation. You want a physician who has the time to know your full story — your history, your lifestyle, your goals — and the expertise to connect the dots that a rushed visit never could.

Overwhelmed by Wellness Culture?

On the other end of the spectrum, there's a world of self-proclaimed health experts, influencer-driven supplement stacks, and expensive protocols with questionable evidence behind them. It's exhausting. And it's expensive — both financially and in terms of the confusion it creates.

You've probably tried some of it. Maybe some of it helped. But you've also probably spent money on things that didn't move the needle, followed advice that contradicted other advice, and felt like you were navigating a maze without a map.

What you actually want is simple: a physician you trust, who understands the science, who knows you personally, and who can cut through the noise to tell you what actually matters for your body.

The Space Between

This membership exists in the space between those two worlds. It combines the clinical rigor and evidence base of traditional medicine with the depth, personalization, and root-cause focus of functional medicine — all within a framework designed to optimize your health for the long term.

No fifteen-minute visits. No influencer pseudoscience. Just a physician-led, science-grounded, deeply personal approach to helping you perform at your best.

What Makes This Membership Different

A few things set this practice apart from both conventional healthcare and the wellness industry:

  • Direct physician access: You work with Dr. O'Bryan directly — not a rotating cast of providers. He knows your name, your history, and your goals.
  • Intentionally small panel: Fewer than 70 patients total. That's not a marketing line — it's a structural commitment to depth over volume.
  • Three disciplines, one plan: Functional, traditional, and longevity medicine woven together into a cohesive, personalized strategy.
  • Access to advanced testing: Comprehensive labs, body composition, metabolic profiling, and more — available through the clinic as part of your care plan, with transparent pricing and no middlemen.
  • 24/7 telehealth access: Because health questions don't wait for business hours.

Is This Right for You?

If you've read this far and found yourself nodding, the answer is probably yes. This membership is built for people who are ready to take their health seriously — not in a fad-diet, quick-fix way, but in a thoughtful, strategic, physician-guided way.

It's for people who want more from their healthcare. More time. More depth. More personalization. More accountability. More results.

Membership is limited by design. If you're interested, the next step is a discovery call — a conversation to see if we're the right fit for each other. No pressure, no pitch. Just an honest conversation about your health and what you're looking for.
